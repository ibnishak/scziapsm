Dr. Heena Chawla

Dr. Heena Chawla is a dedicated and dynamic young medical professional hailing from Kota, Rajasthan. Known for her sincerity, discipline, and strong work ethic, she is currently pursuing her postgraduation in Community Medicine at Government Medical College, Ratlam. She completed her MBBS from R.D. Gardi Medical College, Ujjain, where she developed a keen interest in public health and preventive medicine, alongside building a strong foundation in clinical medicine. Apart from her academic and clinical interests, Dr. Heena also has a great interest in dance, for her, it is not only a hobby but a way to stay connected with herself and maintain balance in her otherwise demanding routine.

Dr. Taniya Jonwal

Dr. Taniya Jonwal hails from the vibrant dunes of Kutch, Gujarat. She completed her undergraduate medical education at Smolensk State Medical University in Russia, gaining not only a solid academic foundation but also valuable international exposure. Upon returning to India, she served diligently under the Gujarat Government for two years, during which she became an active member of the Indian Medical Association. In recognition of her dedication to public service and youth leadership, she was honored in Delhi with the prestigious “Dr. Ketan Desai – National Yuva Leader Award.” Currently, she is pursuing her post-graduation in Community Medicine, driven by a deep desire to make a meaningful difference at the grassroots level. Her vision is to rise through the public health leadership hierarchy, with a strong focus on empowering underserved communities. Outside her medical pursuits, she is also passionate about music and enjoys singing as a creative outlet.
